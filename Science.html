Clay Fossils

Another project we did was making clay fossils! I chose to make crinoids. Crinoids still exist today, they are known as sea lilies and are related to starfish and sea urchins. I tried to shape the clay to look as close as to the reference picture as possible. Making the little rings around the stalk using my nails was the hardest part in this project. The easiest part was the tentacle-like arms it had. I just had to get a small strip of clay and roll it around using the inside of my palms to achieve the look.
